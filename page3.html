Writing Skills

Mathematics Empowerment 

We believe that successful intervention and support in mathematics should be multisensory, encompass content relatable to students and involve repetition, repetition and more repetition! Delivered through the explicit teaching of mathematical concepts, coupled with scaffolded experiences in a supportive, nurturing environment. Furthermore, our program aims to achieve this through the use of engaging, carefully selected materials to suit your child's needs, as well as, various, handy strategies, tools and tricks.
